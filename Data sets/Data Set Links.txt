* https://www.kaggle.com/sobhanmoosavi/us-weather-events

This is a countrywide weather events dataset with more than 5 million events, 
which covers 49 states of the United States. Examples of weather events are rain, 
snow, storm, and freezing condition. The data is collected from January 2016 to 
December 2019, using historical weather reports that exist for airport-based weather 
stations across the country.
